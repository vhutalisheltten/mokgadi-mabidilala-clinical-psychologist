About the Practice

Confidential psychological care in Polokwane and online.

The practice supports adolescents, adults, couples, and families through assessment, psychotherapy, counselling, and adjustment support.

Approach

Care begins with a conversation. The first step is understanding what has brought you here, what feels most urgent, and what kind of support would be useful now.

Sessions may focus on emotional wellbeing, relationships, trauma, grief, academic pressure, work stress, burnout, or major life changes.

Who Can Book

Appointments are available for self-referrals, healthcare practitioner referrals, and organisational referrals.

Consultations can be arranged in person at 288 Marshall Street, Polokwane, or online where appropriate.
