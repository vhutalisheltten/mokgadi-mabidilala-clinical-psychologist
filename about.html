About Mokgadi Mabidilala

Registered Clinical Psychologist providing compassionate psychological care.

Mokgadi Mabidilala is a registered Clinical Psychologist with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA), Practice No: 1254677. She holds a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology and has extensive experience in both private and public practice. She works with children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families, offering support for anxiety, depression, trauma, stress, grief, relationship challenges, and other emotional difficulties in a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental environment where clients are empowered to achieve lasting emotional wellbeing and personal growth.
