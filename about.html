About Mokgadi Mabidilala

Registered Clinical Psychologist providing compassionate psychological care.

Mokgadi Mabidilala is a registered Clinical Psychologist with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA), dedicated to providing compassionate psychological care.

Professional Biography

She holds a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology and has extensive experience in both private and public practice.

She works with children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families, offering support for anxiety, depression, trauma, stress, grief, relationship challenges, and other emotional difficulties.

Mokgadi is committed to creating a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental environment where clients are empowered to achieve lasting emotional wellbeing and personal growth.

Qualifications and Registration

  • Master's degree in Clinical Psychology
  • Registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA)
  • Practice number: 1254677
  • Experience in private and public practice settings

Experience

The practice supports a broad range of emotional, relational, developmental, and adjustment concerns. Care is tailored to each client's needs, goals, background, and pace.
