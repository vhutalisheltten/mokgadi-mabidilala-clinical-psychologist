FAQs

Common questions before booking.

These answers help visitors understand access, referrals, medical aid, and what to expect.

Do I need a referral?

Self-referrals, healthcare practitioner referrals, and organisational referrals can be considered. Contact the practice if you are unsure what applies to your situation.

Are online sessions available?

Yes. Online consultations are available where appropriate and can support clients who cannot attend in person because of location, schedule, travel, or personal circumstances.

Is medical aid accepted?

Medical aids are accepted, including Discovery, Bonitas, Fedhealth, GEMS, Momentum, Medihelp and others. Please contact the practice to confirm benefits and authorisation requirements.

What happens after I send a booking request?

The practice will respond to confirm availability, consultation format, payment options, and next steps.

Who does the practice work with?

Support is available for children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families.
