Services Offered

Care for difficult seasons, decisions, and transitions.

Psychological Assessments

Structured assessment to understand emotional, behavioural, academic, work, or adjustment concerns.

Individual Therapy

A confidential space to explore stress, emotional difficulties, loss, life changes, and self-understanding at your own pace.

Online Therapy

Professional psychological support from the comfort and privacy of your own space, wherever appropriate.

Trauma Debriefings

Guided support after distressing events to process the experience and restore emotional steadiness.

Couples Therapy

A supportive space for partners to strengthen communication, deepen understanding, and build healthier ways of relating.

Family Therapy

Support for families navigating communication, transitions, parenting concerns, boundaries, and emotional strain.

Group Training

Facilitated training for groups and organisations to support communication, coping skills, team wellbeing, and growth.

Who We Serve

Individuals, couples, families, groups, and organisations.

Appointments are available for people seeking self-referral, healthcare practitioner referral, or organisational referral.
