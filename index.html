Services Offered

Care for difficult seasons, decisions, and transitions.

Psychological Assessment

Structured assessment to understand emotional, behavioural, academic, work, or adjustment concerns.

Psychotherapy and Counselling

Supportive, confidential conversations for growth, self-understanding, and healthier coping.

Anxiety, Depression and Stress

Therapy for overwhelm, burnout, low mood, worry, pressure, and emotional exhaustion.

Trauma and Grief Support

A steady space to process loss, painful experiences, and the impact they have on daily life.

Relationships and Family

Help for couples, family concerns, communication patterns, and relational strain.

Life Adjustment

Support for academic, work, identity, and life-stage transitions when things feel unsettled.

Group Counselling

Facilitated group sessions for shared concerns, emotional support, coping skills, and collective growth.

Company Group Assessment

Team-focused group assessment for organisations seeking team building, communication insight, and workplace adjustment support.

Who We Serve

Adolescents, adults, couples, families, groups, and organisations.

Appointments are available for people seeking self-referral, healthcare practitioner referral, or organisational referral.
